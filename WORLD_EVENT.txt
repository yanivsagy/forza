WORLD EVENT: Rogue Motorcyclist

1. How to trigger the event:
	
	The event is triggered by a mouse click on the racetrack.

2. What should happen when the event is triggered:

	When the event is triggered, a motorcycle spawns where the mouse was clicked and oil hazards also spawn on two of the tiles next to where the mouse was clicked.
	The motorcycle will travel in the direction opposite to the direction the rest of the cars are travelling in.

3. What the affected entities should do:
	
	If a car (your own or computer-controlled) lands on the oil, the car will explode and turn into fire.
	If a car (your own or computer-controlled) collides with the motorcycle, the car will explode and turn into fire.

4. What the new entity is and how it should behave

	The new entity is a motorcycle. It moves along the track in the direction opposite to the direction the rest of the cars are travelling in.
	If the motorcycle happens to hit another car, both the motorcycle and the other car will crash and turn into fire.
